# Email Verification (zkEmail)

## ZK Email Verification Overview

ZK Email verification allows users to prove facts about their email accounts using zero-knowledge proofs, without revealing the full email content.

### Key Features

* **Privacy-Preserving**: Only reveal necessary information
* **On-Chain Verification**: Proofs can be verified by smart contracts
* **Receipt Verification**: Verify email receipts and confirmations
