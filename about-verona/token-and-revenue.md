# Token & Revenue (Phase 1)

This page summarizes **Phase 1** token and revenue narrative for developers. It is not financial advice. Figures, partner names, and future phases require marketing and legal sign-off before you repeat them in production apps.

## Phase 1 scope (documentation)

Month 1 docs cover:

* Verona as the intelligence-layer brand
* Network utility on **XION** (staking, fees, ecosystem activity)
* Revenue direction: verification services, application revenue share, marketplace activity, and stablecoin flows contributing to protocol economics

## Naming on chain

| Context | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Docs narrative, product surfaces | **Verona** |
| Chain ID, CLI, addresses, explorers | **XION** |
| Stablecoin products | Follow official announcements only |

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Do not document** unconfirmed revenue numbers, unannounced partners, token migration/swap mechanics, or verUSD-style **yield/interest** language unless explicitly approved in the marketing checklist.
{% endhint %}
